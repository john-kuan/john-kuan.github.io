About Oblique Strategies

The Oblique Strategies is a set of cards created in 1975 by musician Brian Eno and artist Peter Schmidt. Each card offers a cryptic directive or suggestion to aid creativity, particularly in times of creative block. The original copyright belongs to Eno and Schmidt and remains a foundational tool for lateral thinking in the arts.

About Brian Eno

Brian Eno is a renowned musician, producer, and visual artist, celebrated for his pioneering work in ambient music and innovative approaches to art and sound. Eno has collaborated with numerous influential artists and continues to inspire creative disciplines worldwide.

Purpose of This Tool

This ceramic prompt roller is inspired by the spirit of the Oblique Strategies, designed to challenge traditional approaches and spark creativity in pottery. By embracing randomness and experimentation, this tool encourages ceramicists to explore new ideas, overcome creative blocks, and expand their artistic horizons.
